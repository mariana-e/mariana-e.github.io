About M.E.


I'm a Tucson, Arizona native who is an alumn of both The University of Arizona and Pima Community College. My interest in tech was not as immediate as many of those who pursue the field but slowly developed as I started participating in programs in STEM and innovation at both Pima College and the UA eventually leading me to pursue a degree in Management Information Systems. Through my studies I took as many information security courses as were available to me and have been hooked ever since.


I have successfully landed non-technical roles in the tech industry since finishing college but am looking to pursue a more technical role. I am currently applying to grad school, learning wherever I can and seeking an internship or apprenticeship in information security.
